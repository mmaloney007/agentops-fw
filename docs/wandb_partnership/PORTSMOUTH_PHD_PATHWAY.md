## Co-Authored Papers: ALLOWED

**Critical Finding:** You do NOT need to be sole author. The requirements are:

### What Portsmouth Requires

1. **First/Lead Author Preferred:** Candidate should typically be first author or major contributor
2. **Contribution Statements:** For co-authored work, you must outline:
   - Your specific contribution to each paper
   - Percentage contribution (50%+ expected per paper)
   - Whether you were lead author/main contributor

3. **Co-Author Attestations:** Written statements from co-authors confirming your contribution

### How This Works for Your 6-Paper Series

| Paper | Your Role | Collaborator Role | Contribution |
|-------|-----------|-------------------|--------------|
| P1 | First Author | W&B: Technical review | 85%+ |
| P2 | First Author | W&B: Training integration review | 85%+ |
| P3 | First Author | CoreWeave: Benchmark compute | 80%+ |
| P4 | First Author | CoreWeave: MLOps validation | 80%+ |
| P5 | First Author | CoreWeave: Case study data | 75%+ |
| P6 | First Author | Industry consortium review | 70%+ |

**Key:** You remain first author on all papers. Collaborators provide:
- Technical review
- Compute resources
- Validation/testing
- Co-authorship credit proportional to contribution

This is exactly how PhD by Publication works in practice.

## Collaboration Structure

### How to Frame Partnerships for PhD Eligibility

When approaching CoreWeave or W&B:

**DO:**
- Request "contributor" or "co-author" status for collaborators
- Ensure contribution statements specify you did 75%+ of research/writing
- Get written attestations from collaborators about your contribution
- Maintain first authorship on all papers

**DON'T:**
- Give collaborators lead authorship
- Accept "equal contribution" statements
- Let company marketing reframe papers as joint ventures

### Sample Contribution Statement

```
Paper 1: SpecSLOEval — Evaluation Framework

Author Contributions:
- M. Maloney: Conceived research question, designed evaluation framework,
  implemented all code, ran experiments, wrote manuscript (85%)
- W&B Contributor: Reviewed W&B integration patterns, validated best
  practices, provided feedback on experiment logging (10%)
- Supervisor (TBD): Academic guidance and manuscript review (5%)

M. Maloney is corresponding author and takes responsibility for the
integrity of the research.
```

## Registration Pathway

### Step 1: Pre-Application (Now - Feb 2026)
- Complete Papers P1 + P2
- Submit to arXiv/venues
- Identify potential Portsmouth supervisor

### Step 2: Application (Mar-Apr 2026)
- Submit to Computing postgraduate research
- Include:
  - CV and publication list
  - 500-word research summary
  - Proposed supervisor (if known)
  - Draft contribution statements

### Step 3: Registration (Apr-May 2026)
- Formal enrollment
- Confirm supervisor
- Begin 6-12 month clock

### Step 4: Submission (Oct 2026 - Apr 2027)
- Submit portfolio: P1-P6 papers
- Submit 5,000-10,000 word commentary
- Contribution statements for all co-authored papers

### Step 5: Examination (Nov 2026 - Jun 2027)
- Viva voce examination
- Respond to examiner questions
- Award of degree
